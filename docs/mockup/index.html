Meet the Maker

Yarn, tea, and a whole lot of heart.

I started crocheting on a rainy afternoon, and somehow never put the hook down. What began as a hobby turned into a tiny studio of softies, scrunchies, and keepsakes — every piece designed slowly, by hand, in pastel palettes I can't help but reach for.

If you find something here that makes you smile, that's exactly the idea. ♡
